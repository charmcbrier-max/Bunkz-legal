Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Bunkz Community!

Bunkz is built on respect, honesty, and helping each other find great roommates. These guidelines help keep our community safe and positive for everyone.

By using Bunkz, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in account warnings, suspension, or permanent ban.

Our Core Values

  • Respect: Treat others how you want to be treated
  • Honesty: Be truthful in your profile and interactions
  • Safety: Look out for yourself and others
  • Inclusivity: Everyone deserves a safe place to live

1. Be Respectful

✓ DO:

  • Be kind and courteous
  • Respond politely even if not interested
  • Respect people's boundaries
  • Use appropriate language
  • Give others the benefit of the doubt

✗ DON'T:

  • Send harassing or abusive messages
  • Use hate speech or slurs
  • Bully or intimidate others
  • Make threats of any kind
  • Spam users with repeated messages

2. Be Honest

✓ DO:

  • Use your real name and photos
  • Be truthful about your habits
  • Accurately describe your lifestyle
  • Update your profile if things change
  • Communicate your expectations clearly

✗ DON'T:

  • Use someone else's photos
  • Lie about your age or student status
  • Create fake profiles
  • Catfish or impersonate others
  • Misrepresent your intentions

3. No Discrimination

Bunkz is for everyone. Discrimination, harassment, or hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic is strictly prohibited.

Zero Tolerance: We take discrimination seriously. Violations will result in immediate account termination.

Legal Note: While you can have personal preferences for roommates (e.g., gender preference for shared bedroom), publicly discriminatory statements or harassment are not allowed.

4. Use Bunkz for Roommate Finding Only

✓ Appropriate Use:

  • Finding a roommate or roommate group
  • Discussing housing preferences and lifestyle
  • Coordinating housing logistics with matches
  • Building a compatible living arrangement

✗ Inappropriate Use:

  • ❌ Using Bunkz as a dating app
  • ❌ Soliciting romantic or sexual relationships
  • ❌ Promoting businesses or selling products
  • ❌ Asking for money or financial assistance
  • ❌ Recruiting for MLMs, clubs, or organizations
  • ❌ Posting political or religious propaganda
Note: If you're looking to date, use a dating app! Bunkz is specifically for finding roommates. Romantic advances are not appropriate here.

5. Keep Content Appropriate

Profile Photos:

✓ Good Profile Photos:

  • Clear photo of your face
  • Fully clothed
  • Recent photos (within 2 years)
  • Your actual photos (not stock images)
  • Appropriate for all audiences

✗ Prohibited Photos:

  • Nudity or sexually suggestive content
  • Violence or weapons
  • Drugs or illegal substances
  • Offensive symbols or gestures
  • Someone else's photos

Messages & Bios:

  • ✓ Keep language clean and respectful
  • ✓ Focus on roommate compatibility
  • ✗ No sexual content or explicit language
  • ✗ No sharing of personal contact info in bio (share in DMs after matching)

6. Safety First

🛡️ Safety Tips:

  • Meet in Public: First meetings should always be in public places (coffee shop, campus center, library)
  • Tell Someone: Let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting
  • Trust Your Gut: If something feels off, it probably is. Don't ignore red flags.
  • Video Chat First: Consider a video call before meeting in person to verify identity
  • Don't Send Money: Never send money to someone you haven't met in person
  • Protect Your Info: Don't share your full address, SSN, or financial info
  • Verify Student Status: Look for the verification badge (✓) indicating confirmed .edu email
Scam Alert: Be cautious of anyone who:
  • Asks for money before meeting
  • Refuses to video chat or meet in person
  • Has inconsistent information in their profile
  • Pressures you to make quick decisions
  • Asks you to communicate off-platform immediately
Report suspicious activity immediately.

7. Privacy & Consent

  • ✓ Respect others' privacy in conversations
  • ✓ Ask before sharing someone's contact info
  • ✗ Don't screenshot and share private conversations without consent
  • ✗ Don't share someone's profile outside of Bunkz without permission
  • ✗ Don't use information from Bunkz to contact people on other platforms (without their consent)

8. Groups Best Practices

If You're Creating a Group:

  • Be clear about group size and what you're looking for
  • Respond to applicants in a timely manner
  • Communicate expectations with group members
  • Be fair when accepting new members

If You're Applying to a Group:

  • Read the group's preferences before applying
  • Be patient waiting for a response
  • Respect the group's decision if not accepted
  • Communicate openly with group members

9. Reporting & Blocking

When to Report:

  • Harassment or abusive behavior
  • Fake profiles or catfishing
  • Inappropriate content (sexual, violent, etc.)
  • Scams or solicitation
  • Discrimination or hate speech
  • Underage users (under 18)
  • Any safety concerns

How to Report:

  1. Tap the "..." menu on any profile or message
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose the reason for reporting
  4. Add any additional details
  5. Submit report

We review all reports within 24-48 hours. Serious violations are addressed immediately.

Blocking:

You can block any user at any time. Blocked users:

  • Won't see your profile in their deck
  • Can't message you
  • Won't appear in your deck
  • Won't know they've been blocked

10. Consequences for Violations

Depending on the severity of the violation, we may:

First Offense (Minor):

  • Warning message
  • Required review of Community Guidelines
  • Temporary restriction of certain features

Serious or Repeat Offenses:

  • Account suspension (7-30 days)
  • Permanent ban from Bunkz
  • Report to university (if applicable)
  • Report to law enforcement (for illegal activity)

Immediate Ban (Zero Tolerance):

  • Threats or violence
  • Sexual harassment or explicit content
  • Hate speech or severe discrimination
  • Being under 18 years old
  • Scamming or financial fraud
  • Doxxing or sharing private information
Appeals: If you believe your account was banned in error, contact support@bunkzapp.com within 30 days. Include your username and explanation. We'll review and respond within 5 business days.

11. What We Don't Moderate

Bunkz is not responsible for:

  • Personal disagreements between users
  • Roommate compatibility after you move in together
  • Housing contracts or lease agreements
  • Financial disputes between roommates
  • Issues arising from in-person meetings

These are personal matters between users. We provide the platform to connect, but you're responsible for your decisions.

12. Help Us Build a Better Community

You can help by:

  • ✓ Reporting policy violations when you see them
  • ✓ Being a positive, respectful member
  • ✓ Giving feedback on how we can improve
  • ✓ Welcoming new users
  • ✓ Sharing success stories

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about these guidelines or need help:

Report a User: Use in-app reporting feature

General Questions: support@bunkzapp.com

Safety Concerns: safety@bunkzapp.com

Urgent Threats: Contact local law enforcement immediately, then contact us
